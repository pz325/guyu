Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)

TCM has existed over 2000 years. It is a comprehensive medical system based on its own philosophy, with traditional diagnosis and therapeutic methods.

There are three basic components in TCM: Chinese Herbal Remedies, Acupuncture and Tui Na (Therapeutic Massage)

Chinese Medicine theory believes human being are children of nature. The spirit of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) is to achieve the harmonisation of human and nature. TCM treatment is a natural therapeutic method to maintain the body’s equilibrium, promote body healing and improve functions systematically. TCM can be an effective therapy to restore body balance and promote both physical and mental harmony.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a form of therapy in which insertion of sterile, disposable, stainless steel fine needles applies on the acupuncture points of the body, to promote the flow of Qi (or vital energy), thereby restoring and maintaining health. Acupuncture is widely accepted and used globally.

Tui Na

Tui Na - Therapeutic Massage - is a widely used treatment method for both relaxation and therapy. Paediatric massage is well accepted and commonly used for infants, toddlers and young children. Paediatric massage therapy is suitable for children aged under 12.

Other Treatments

Electro-acupuncture, Cupping, Guasha (Scrapping), Moxa, Ear seeds therapies are also available.

About Dr. Yu Gu

Dr. Yu Gu, BA, BSC, Ph.D., MBAcC, is a fully qualified acupuncturist, insured and accredited by the British Acupuncture Council. Yu is committed to working to professional guidelines and adhering to strict clinical hygiene standards.

Dr. Yu Gu obtained a Bachelor’s degree in Medicine in 2000. She was awarded the Master Degree of Acupuncture in Chinese Medicine in 2003. Since then she moved to the UK and practiced in the Five Element Clinic. Yu achieved a Ph.D. in Biomedical Sciences at the University of Aberdeen in 2007. In 2019, she achieved the second Bachelor’s degree after 3.5 years of study and practice in the College of Integrated Chinese Medicine, UK.

Dr. Yu Gu is the third generation of a traditional Chinese medicine family who practices TCM for more than a century. Grandfather (1904-2003) – Dr. Mingsan Gu was a nationally reputable Chinese medicine herbalist who practiced until 95 years old. He was officially awarded as one of the top 10 Distinguished TCM Doctors (十大名医) by the Chinese Ministry of Health in 1991. His expertise was to treat a wide variety of difficult conditions including cancer, gynaecology, internal diseases, etc.

Dr. Yu Gu’s father Dr. Yanfang Gu inherited the wealth of knowledge from grandfather and has practiced more than 40 years with a good reputation nationally. He has a wide range of expertise treating difficult conditions, and also specialised at treating cancer & renal patients.

As the 3rd generation of the TCM family, Yu is passionate about acupuncture. Yu has inherited the TCM knowledge and experience from her family and has years of experience in the clinic. She enjoys treating a wide variety of patients and conditions using both TCM and Five Elements approaches to achieve the wellbeing of physical and mental levels.
